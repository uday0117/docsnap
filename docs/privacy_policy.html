Privacy Policy for DocSnap

Last Updated: May 27, 2025

Introduction

UK Solutions ("us", "we", or "our") operates the DocSnap mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal information.

DocSnap is a document scanning and PDF generation application that operates entirely locally on your device. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your usage
  • Store your documents on our servers
  • Transmit document data to external servers
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Require account registration or login
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Request unnecessary permissions

Data Storage

All scanned documents, PDFs, images, and app data are stored locally on your device only. This means:

  • Your documents remain private on your device
  • No documents or images are uploaded to any server
  • Your scanned data is not accessible to us or anyone else
  • You have complete control over your documents
  • Data is not backed up to cloud services by the app
  • Documents are saved to your device's storage with your explicit permission

Permissions

DocSnap requires the following permissions to function:

  • Camera: To capture images of documents for scanning
  • Storage/Photos (Android 13+): To save scanned documents and PDFs to your device
  • Storage (Android 12 and below): To save and access your scanned documents

We only request permissions that are essential for the app's core functionality. All permissions are used solely for document scanning and saving features.

Camera Usage

DocSnap uses your device camera to:

  • Capture images of documents
  • Scan receipts, invoices, and papers
  • Process images locally on your device

Important: All camera images are processed entirely on your device. No images are sent to external servers or cloud services.

Document Processing

DocSnap processes your documents locally:

  • Image Enhancement: Applies filters and enhancements on-device
  • PDF Generation: Converts scanned images to PDF locally
  • OCR (Text Recognition): If enabled, performs text recognition locally using on-device ML models
  • Crop & Edit: All editing operations happen on your device

No document data leaves your device during any processing operation.

Sharing Documents

DocSnap allows you to share documents:

  • Sharing is initiated only when you explicitly choose to share
  • Documents are shared directly from your device using Android's built-in share functionality
  • We do not intercept, store, or track shared documents
  • Sharing destination apps (email, messaging, cloud storage) have their own privacy policies

Third-Party Services

DocSnap does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or cloud services that may collect information used to identify you.

Data Security

Since all your documents and data are stored locally on your device:

  • Your data security depends on your device's security
  • We strongly recommend using device lock features (PIN, password, fingerprint, face ID)
  • If you uninstall the app, documents saved to device storage remain on your device
  • App-specific data (settings, history) will be deleted when you uninstall
  • We do not have access to recover your data if it's lost
  • Consider backing up important documents to your preferred secure storage

Children's Privacy

Our Service is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone, including children.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, you maintain complete control:

  • All documents and data belong to you
  • You can delete app data by clearing app data in system settings
  • Documents saved to device storage can be managed through file managers
  • No data is retained by us after app deletion
  • No data is shared with any third parties
  • You control when and how documents are shared

Document Retention

Documents you scan with DocSnap:

  • Are saved to your device's storage folders with your permission
  • Remain on your device even after uninstalling the app
  • Can be managed, moved, or deleted using your device's file manager
  • Are stored in standard formats (JPEG, PNG, PDF) readable by any compatible app

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
